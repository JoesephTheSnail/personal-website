Mindset Matters is a monthly newsletter where I take one idea — about mindset, habits, or personal growth — and break it down through personal experience, research, and practical takeaways.

The goal is simple: help people think better about themselves and the way they move through life.

I launched in March 2025 and have published 12 issues since, covering topics from imposter syndrome and dedication to self-love, leadership, and how to fail correctly. A new issue goes out every month on Substack.
